INTRODUCTION

Get to know us

Located on the southeastern coast of Vietnam, Vung Tau appears like a precious gem shining amidst the blue sea. With its captivating coastal beauty and serene atmosphere, it is an ideal destination for those seeking to escape the hustle and bustle of life and enjoy moments of tranquility by the seaside.

With its peak often shrouded in morning mist, Big Mountain offers an exciting retreat for wanderers. The trails leading to the top wind through cool forests where the songs of birds blend with the distant sound of waves. At the summit, a panoramic view unfolds, with the deep blue sea stretching endlessly, and the city below appearing tiny amidst the vastness. Small Mountain - Moments of Serenity by the Sea: Small Mountain is like a familiar companion that offers peaceful moments amidst life's chaos. Ascending its slopes, you'll feel the gentle sea breeze brushing against your skin and discover vibrant flowers along the pathways. From its summit, the whole beach sprawls beneath, presenting a vibrant tableau beneath the open sky. Front Beach - Merging with the Ocean's Rhythm: Front Beach, with its smooth sandy shore, stretches along the coast, inviting leisurely strolls. Under the warm sun, the sand shimmers like pearls, and the gentle waves lull the shore in a rhythmic embrace. This is where you pause, leaving behind life's burdens, and immerse yourself in the embrace of the deep blue sea. Back Beach - A Private Space by the Ocean: Tucked beside the ocean, Back Beach offers a secluded haven, untouched and poetic. As the sun sets, it casts an orange and purple hue across the water, creating a mystic scene. Here, you stand still, feeling deeply connected to nature, inhaling the crisp sea breeze. These enchanting spots in Vung Tau - Big Mountain, Small Mountain, Front Beach, and Back Beach - vividly display the allure of Vietnam's southeastern coastal beauty. A journey here promises an unforgettable experience for nature enthusiasts and those seeking the soothing embrace of the sea.
