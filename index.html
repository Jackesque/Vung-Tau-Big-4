INTRODUCTION

Get to know us

Located on the southeastern coast of Viet Nam, Vung Tau appears like a precious gem shining amidst the blue sea. With its captivating coastal beauty and serene atmosphere, it is an ideal destination for those seeking to escape the hustle and bustle of life and enjoy moments of tranquility by the seaside.

These enchanting spots in Vung Tau - Núi Lớn, Núi Nhỏ, Bãi Trước, Bãi Sau - vividly display the allure of Viet Nam's southeastern coastal beauty. A journey here promises an unforgettable experience for nature enthusiasts and those seeking the soothing embrace of the sea.

Núi lớn

Tranquil Time Unfolds

With its peak often shrouded in morning mist, Núi Lớn offers an exciting retreat for wanderers. The trails leading to the top wind through cool forests where the songs of birds blend with the distant sound of waves. At the summit, a panoramic view unfolds, with the deep blue sea stretching endlessly, and the city below appearing tiny amidst the vastness.

Núi nhỏ

Moments of Serenity by the Sea

Núi Nhỏ is like a familiar companion that offers peaceful moments amidst life's chaos. Ascending its slopes, you'll feel the gentle sea breeze brushing against your skin and discover vibrant flowers along the pathways. From its summit, the whole beach sprawls beneath, presenting a vibrant tableau beneath the open sky.

Bãi trước

Merging with the Ocean's Rhythm

Bãi Trước, with its smooth sandy shore, stretches along the coast, inviting leisurely strolls. Under the warm sun, the sand shimmers like pearls, and the gentle waves lull the shore in a rhythmic embrace. This is where you pause, leaving behind life's burdens, and immerse yourself in the embrace of the deep blue sea.

Bãi sau

A Private Space by the Ocean

Tucked beside the ocean, Bãi Sau offers a secluded haven, untouched and poetic. As the sun sets, it casts an orange and purple hue across the water, creating a mystic scene. Here, you stand still, feeling deeply connected to nature, inhaling the crisp sea breeze.
